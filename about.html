About Me

This is what I do.

Welcome to my personal website! I'm Ina, a curious and adventurous individual with a passion for technology and travel. I completed my Bachelor's degree in engineering from PES University, Bangalore, where I developed a strong foundation in various engineering disciplines. Throughout my studies, I discovered a deep fascination for emerging technologies and their potential to shape the world we live in. Post my undergrad, I worked at Cisco for two and a half years as a Product Engineer

When I'm not immersed in the world of technology, you'll often find me exploring new destinations and embracing different cultures. Traveling has broadened my horizons, allowing me to appreciate the beauty of our diverse world while fostering my personal growth. I believe that exploration and experiencing different environments play a crucial role in expanding our perspectives and unlocking our true potential.

Through this website, I aim to share my knowledge, experiences, and insights in the field of technology and travel. Here, you'll find a collection of articles, travel guides, and resources that reflect my enthusiasm for these two distinct yet intertwined passions. I hope that my content will not only inspire and entertain but also provide valuable information to fellow technology enthusiasts and fellow wanderers alike.

Thank you for visiting, and I look forward to connecting with you!
